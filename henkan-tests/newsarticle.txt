Roshia daitouryoufu ha Ukuraina ga mujinki de pu-chin daitouryou wo neratte kougekishiyou to shita to shuchoushi, sousa wo tsuduketeimasu. sarani daitouryoufu ha" Amerika no shiji ni shitagatta mono da" toshite kougeki ni ha Amerika mo kannyoshita toshite hihan wo tsuyometeimasu. Roshia daitouryoufu ha 3 nichi, 2 ki no mujinki ga Mosukuwa no Kuremurin ni aru daitouryoufu wo kougekishiyou to shita to happyoushi, pu-chin daitouryou wo neratta tero koui da to shuchoushite, sousa wo tsuduketeimasu. daitouryoufu no pesukofu houdoukan ha 4 nichi, Ukuraina no zerensuki- seiken niyoru mono da to shuchoushita ue de" Amerika no shiji nishitagatte Ukuraina ga kougeki no shudan to hyouteki wo erandeiru koto wo, wareware ha shitteiru" to nobe, Amerika mo kannyoshita toshite, hihan wo tsuyometeimasu
